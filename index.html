FAQs.

Alpine Bus Journeys?
Forget crowded cities! Alpine Bus Journeys are your comfy seat with panoramic windows to breathtaking mountain vistas, charming villages, and air so fresh it might make you yodel (please don't, unless you're outside). It's transportation with a side of unparalleled scenery (and maybe a hairpin turn).
What about seating options?
Our buses feature comfortable, ergonomic seating with extra legroom. Choose from standard seats, premium seats with additional recline, or wheelchair-accessible spaces. All seats include individual reading lights and USB charging ports.
What is the Alpine Wallet?
The Alpine Wallet is our digital payment system that allows you to store credit, purchase tickets, and earn rewards. Load money once and use it for all your Alpine Bus journey needs, including onboard purchases.
Why a Bus Station Fee?
Bus station fees help maintain our terminals, provide security, cleaning services, and passenger amenities. This ensures a safe, comfortable waiting environment with clean facilities and helpful staff.

Why are buses late?
Bus delays can occur due to traffic conditions, weather, mechanical issues, or passenger boarding delays. We monitor all routes in real-time and provide updates through our mobile app and station displays.
